WIP PROJECT: feel/

this started out as a graphic design exercise i created to help express difficult feelings. i ended up loving the results and decided to make them into lenticular prints, turning my digital gifs into physical objects. will having these tangible objects in my hands make it easier for me to process these feelings? i don't know, but here's my heart on my sleeve.
