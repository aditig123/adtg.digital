PROJECT

Micro beauty logo and packaging design

CONCEPT

Created for a near future where the overuse of antibiotics wipe out bacteria from Earth. Human immune systems, which rely on bacteria, become almost non-existent. Micro beauty has created a lotion with microbacteria to apply directly on the skin, with promises of immune system restoration. The brand advertises 3.5 million* microbacteria per bottle.

*3.5 million isn’t that much, this is a critique of the beauty and health industry.
