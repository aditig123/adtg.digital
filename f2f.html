PROJECT

Campaign Identity for Face to Face

CONCEPT

Face to Face is an ethnographic look at contemporary America. This campaign aims to bring individuals together from across the country to talk about race, family, gender, socio-economics, and religion. These interactions are digitized and showcased on our website. We hope from one on one interviews we can spread different viewpoints that make America the country it is today. Logo, posters, business system, t-shirt, and website were created for the campaign.
