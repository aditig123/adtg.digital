PROJECT

Bacteria Letterforms

CONCEPT

Communicating with bacteria*

METHOD 1 "STREAKING"

This method was done by streaking samples of bacteria onto agar petri dishes.

Why this failed: The petri dishes we not sterilized causing bacteria to grow in unwanted locations. The dishes were stored in a room slightly lower than optimal temperature for bacterial growth therefore causing some letters to underdevelop.

METHOD 2 "PAINTING WITH E.COLI"

This method was done in a lab setting with dormant e.coli bacteria engineered to produce pigmented proteins. The bacteria was painted onto sterile agar petri dishes. They are stored in the lab in a more controlled, bacteria friendly temperature.

APPLICATION 1 “MICRO BEAUTY INTRO REEL”

Method 2 involving engineered pigment producing e.coli is an innovative communication method. This aligns well with micro beauty and it's mission to innovate in the health and beauty industry.

*inspired by CONTAGION BACTERIA BILLBOARD
