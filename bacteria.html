project:
bacteria letterforms

concept:
creating letterforms with bacteria through various methods.*

method 1 “streaking”
this method was done by streaking samples of bacteria onto agar petri dishes.
why this failed: the petri dishes we not 100% sterilized causing bacteria to grow in unwanted locations. The petri dishes were stored in a room slightly lower than optimal temperature for bacterial growth therefore causing some letters to underdevelop.

method 2 “painting with e.coli”
this method was done in a lab setting with dormant e.coli bacteria engineered to produce pigmented proteins. the bacteria was painted onto sterile agar petri dishes. they are stored in the lab in a more controlled, bacteria friendly temperature.

*inspired by contagion baterica billboard
